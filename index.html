Malaysia's First
ISN Certified Gym

We are the first and only gym in Malaysia officially certified by the Institut Sukan Negara (ISN). Our facility meets the same standards required for national athletes to train, prepare, and perform at their peak.

When you train here, you're training at a place trusted and recognized on the national stage.
